Part 2: The Timeline

Evolutionary Roadmap

1969: UNIX

Born at Bell Labs. Created by Ken Thompson & Dennis Ritchie. The foundation of modern multitasking.

1991: LINUX

The Open Source Revolution. Linus Torvalds made power accessible to everyone, for free.

2008: ANDROID

The Mobile Era. Bringing the power of the Linux kernel to billions of smartphones.

Why UNIX was Great

Technological Breakthroughs

C

Portability via C

By rewriting UNIX in the C language, it became the first OS that could easily be ported to different hardware types.

Everything is a File

A brilliant abstraction where hardware, memory, and networking are treated as simple files for consistent management.
